Tame the Web, Enhance your Journey.

Zenify is your all-in-one extension for a distraction-free, customized browsing experience. Block annoyances, focus on content, and tweak websites to fit your needs. It's your web, your way.

Features

  • Content Blocking: Block unwanted elements like ads, videos, images, and more.
  • Page Customization: Delete, disable, and add HTML elements for a tailored browsing experience.
  • Style Control: Fine-tune website aesthetics with the ability to add, delete, or disable CSS rules.
  • Script Management: Inject custom JavaScript or disable existing website scripts.
  • Privacy and Security: Block trackers, scripts, and potentially risky content for a more secure browsing experience.
  • Multiple Modes: Enter "Zen Mode" for a text-only view or "Grayscale Mode" to minimize distractions.
  • Import/Export Settings: Easily share your customized settings across devices.
